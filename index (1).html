About Us

All the information you need about us

Foundation

Founded in 2016, Knacksource Consulting & Training Services is a full service international human resources management consulting firm with office in the Bangalore, Karnataka. This Company offers strategic recruiting, training, and consulting services mostly in Bangalore and also serves entire Karnataka. Knacksource Consulting & Training Services helps organizations leverage the power of their people to achieve peak company performance.

Work

Because strategic human resources is one of the last competitive advantages that exists for many industries, Ankush HR Services features a full range of services to help companies. Hire the very best person for each job Increase employee performance through skillbased training and development and Maximize organizational effectiveness through human resources outsourcing.

Approach

Company's unique approach combines two core competencies of business process: outsourcing and performance management. Our process is supported by specialists in the behavioral and industrial sciences and by human resource management experts.


Mission

We ask our clients the right questions, acquire critical information, anticipate emerging needs, and apply our expertise to influence the best human capital outcomes. We make it possible for remarkable people to achieve remarkable results.

Vision

To be the leading human resources consulting company in Karnataka and to be a leading workplace diversity consulting firm.

Values

  • Perseverance
  • Belief in the human potential of the Indian continent
